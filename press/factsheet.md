# Vitrine — Factsheet

**Product:** Vitrine
**Tagline:** For miniature painters
**One-sentence pitch:** A single app on iOS and Android that catalogues your collection, saves your paint recipes, tracks your paints, and shares what you've finished.

## Pricing

- **Free** — every feature, capped at 20 miniatures and 10 recipes (units of the same model count as one miniature)
- **Premium** — unlimited miniatures and recipes
  - $3.99 / month
  - $19.99 / year
  - $34.99 lifetime (one-time purchase)
- **Pre-order discount:** Pre-ordering Vitrine in the App Store or Google Play unlocks 50% off any Premium plan, for as long as you keep it.

## The pitch in one paragraph

Most hobby tools cover a slice of the painter's workflow. Vitrine brings the pieces together. Track a squad piece by piece instead of pretending the whole unit moves as one. Record exact paints and mix ratios for every step you paint, then run a Session against that recipe on your next model. Tag everything with manufacturer, setting, model, and genre, and use those tags to discover what other painters in the community are building.

## Key features

1. Per-piece pipeline tracking across customisable stages
2. Paint recipes with mix ratios, step photos, and version history
3. Guided and freeform painting sessions
4. Personal paint library with cross-brand equivalents, barcode scanning, and PaintRack import
5. Public showroom at app.vitrineminis.com/u/your-handle
6. Discover feed for browsing community miniatures by tag
7. Statistics hub with shareable infographics

## The maker

Johan Forsell, a Swedish indie developer who picked up miniature painting recently and couldn't find an app where everything came together. Built Vitrine to be the one he wanted.
