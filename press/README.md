## Long description (about 200 words)

Vitrine is a native app for self-painting miniature hobbyists, built for both iOS and Android. It catalogues every model in your collection piece by piece, saves the paint recipes you write, walks you through painting sessions step by step, keeps a personal paint inventory that knows cross-brand alternatives, and gives every painter a public showroom for showing off finished work.

Most hobby tools cover a slice of the workflow. A paint inventory app here, a painting timer there, social posts somewhere else. Vitrine brings those pieces into one place. Track a 5-pack squad at 2 finished, 1 work-in-progress, and 2 primed at the same time, instead of pretending the whole unit moves as one. Record the exact paints and mix ratios you used on each step, then run a Session against that recipe when you paint the next model. Tag everything with manufacturer, setting, model, and genre, and use those tags to discover what other painters in the community are building.

Vitrine launches on the App Store and Google Play on June 8, 2026, with a Free tier that includes every feature and a Premium upgrade that lifts the 20-miniature and 10-recipe caps. Pre-ordering the app in either store before launch unlocks 50% off any Premium plan, whether monthly, yearly, or the one-time lifetime purchase.

## Key features

- **Per-piece pipeline tracking.** Every miniature is tracked piece by piece across customisable stages, so a squad can sit at multiple statuses at once.
- **Paint recipes that travel.** Structured, step-by-step recipes with mix ratios, step photos, and version history. Pull any public recipe into your own library, or publish yours for other painters.
- **Guided painting sessions.** Walk through a saved recipe step by step, or record a freeform session as you paint and save it as a new recipe at the end.
- **Smart paint library.** Cross-brand equivalents from community conversion charts, barcode scanning, bulk range imports, PaintRack import, colour-similarity sorting, and user-submitted paints with a pending review queue.
- **Public showrooms.** Every named account gets a shareable handle and a public profile at app.vitrineminis.com/u/your-handle, with link previews and Universal Links into the app.
- **Discover feed.** A chronological feed of public miniatures from the community, filterable by manufacturer, setting, model, or genre.
- **Statistics hub.** Six built-in charts (State Timeline, Pile of Shame, Collection Comparison, Time in State, Completion Rate, Painting Velocity), each exportable as a branded infographic.
