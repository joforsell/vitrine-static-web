Showroom Challenge

Pre-launch giveaway for painters. Post a short "Top 3 Contenders" video, tag us, and you're in the draw. Three random entrants take home a lifetime Premium subscription, drawn after the app ships.

  1. Reserve your handle first.

    Lock in your handle on the reservation page before recording, so it's the one you can show in your video.

  2. Post a "Top 3 Contenders" video.

    A short clip showing the three of your minis you'd put up for the Showroom Spotlight at launch. TikTok or Instagram Reels. Drop your full showroom URL in the caption (app.vitrineminis.com/u/your-handle) so viewers know where to find your work once Vitrine ships.

  3. Tag @vitrineminis or DM us.

    Tag us in the post or send a DM with a link to your video. Either works. We pick three random entrants for the lifetime Premium draw after launch.
