Privacy Policy

Vitrine

Effective date: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Vitrine mobile application and the website at vitrineminis.com (together, the "Service"), operated by Johan Forsell ("we", "us", "our"), collect, use, and protect your information.

1. What we collect

Account information. When you create a named account we collect your email address, display name, and chosen handle. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the name and email address associated with that account.

Content you create. Miniature data, photos, paint recipes, painting session records, paint collection data, and any other content you add to the Service.

Usage data. We collect basic analytics to understand how the Service is used, including app opens, feature usage, and error reports. This data is aggregated and not tied to your identity.

Device information. Device type, operating system version, and app version, used for compatibility and debugging purposes.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the Service
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your public showroom and shared content to other users
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • To improve the Service based on aggregated usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. How we share your information

Public content. If you create a public showroom, your display name, handle, bio, and any collections or miniatures you mark as public are visible to anyone on the web.

Service providers. We use the following third-party services to operate Vitrine:

  • Supabase for authentication, database hosting, file storage, and server-side functions
  • Apple / Google for sign-in, if you choose those methods
  • Apple App Store for processing Vitrine Premium subscription payments
  • RevenueCat for managing subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Sentry for crash reporting and error tracking
  • TelemetryDeck for privacy-friendly, anonymous usage analytics

These providers only access your data as needed to perform their services and are bound by their own privacy policies.

Legal requirements. We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase processes data in accordance with their privacy and security practices. We choose hosting regions that comply with applicable data protection regulations.

5. How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and content within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@vitrineminis.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Cookies and tracking

The Vitrine website does not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts. The mobile app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach us at hello@vitrineminis.com.
